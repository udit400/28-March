Frequently Asked Questions

What is deepfake image detection?
Deepfake image detection uses AI to figure out whether an image has been manipulated or generated artificially. The analysis checks for lighting glitches, strange textures, metadata issues, and other forensic clues.
Is the deepfake detector 100% accurate?
No detector is perfect. Results should be treated as a strong signal, not an absolute guarantee. That's why the report includes both a score and an explanation.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Images are processed by your backend and not stored permanently by the page. You can scan sensitive files without creating an account.
What file types does the product support?
The page supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. The current UI is designed around a 3 MB limit for quick uploads and fast scans.
How do I interpret the detection results?
You'll get an overall forgery score, a verdict, and a plain-language explanation. Lower scores suggest the image looks authentic, while higher scores mean more suspicious patterns were detected.
