Privacy Policy

We built Deepfake Detection to help people verify suspicious media while keeping their data exposure as low as possible.

Last Updated: July 27, 2025
We do not want authenticity checks to become a privacy risk of their own. This policy explains what we process, why we process it, and how we keep that handling limited.

1. Who We Are

Deepfake Detection is an AI-powered media verification service focused on manipulated images, video, and audio. If you have privacy questions, you can reach us at hello@deepfakedetection.io.

2. What We Collect

Your Uploaded Files

Media files are processed to generate an authenticity result and related reporting. The product is designed around short-lived handling rather than long-term content retention.

Basic Usage Information

We may keep aggregate service-level statistics such as request volume, error rates, or feature usage to operate and improve the detector.

Technical Data

Like any web service, we may temporarily process IP address, browser information, and device details needed for delivery, reliability, and abuse prevention.

What We Do Not Intend To Collect

  • Unnecessary personal profile data such as your home address or phone number.
  • Long-term copies of uploaded media after normal processing completes.
  • Advertising profiles built from your detection activity.

3. How We Use Data

  • To analyze uploaded content and return authenticity scores, summaries, and detailed reports.
  • To maintain uptime, investigate bugs, and improve detector quality over time.
  • To adapt the system as new manipulation techniques appear.

4. How Long We Keep Data

Uploaded content is intended to be processed temporarily. Operational logs and aggregate metrics may be retained for limited periods where needed for support, security, and service monitoring.

5. Legal Basis

Where privacy laws such as GDPR apply, our processing is generally based on providing the service you requested, maintaining the security of the product, and improving detection quality in our legitimate interest.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region, you may be able to request access, deletion, correction, restriction, or export of personal data associated with your use of the service.

7. AI and Automated Decisions

The detector evaluates uploaded media for signs of manipulation. It does not make decisions about you as an individual, and you remain responsible for how you interpret or act on the result.

8. Cookies and Analytics

Essential website functions may require technical cookies or equivalent storage. If analytics are used, they should be limited to operating and improving the site rather than building invasive behavioral profiles.

9. Who We Share Data With

We aim to keep sharing minimal. Data may be processed by secure infrastructure or protection partners only where needed to run the service, defend against abuse, or comply with legal obligations.

10. Children’s Privacy

The service is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly seek personal data from children.

11. Incident Response

If a material security event affects personal data, we intend to respond promptly, investigate the scope, and make legally required notifications where applicable.

12. International Transfers

If data is processed across borders, we aim to use appropriate safeguards consistent with the applicable privacy framework.

13. How We Protect Data

  • Encryption in transit and protected service infrastructure.
  • Limited retention and controlled access paths.
  • Operational practices designed to reduce unnecessary exposure of uploaded media.

14. Policy Changes

We may revise this policy as the product changes. When we do, the date above will be updated to reflect the latest version.

15. California Privacy Rights

If California privacy laws apply to you, you may have additional rights concerning disclosure, deletion, and non-discrimination. We do not intend to sell personal information.

16. Contact Us About Privacy

For privacy questions, email support@deepfakedetection.io or privacy@deepfakedetection.io. We will review requests and respond as reasonably quickly as possible.
