Frequently Asked Questions

What is deepfake video detection?
Deepfake video detection is the process of analyzing a video to check if parts of it have been digitally manipulated. It looks for fake faces, voice changes, lip-sync mismatches, and timing issues.
Is the deepfake video detector 100% accurate?
No detector is perfect, but the tool combines visual, audio, and timing analysis to stay as reliable as possible. Use the report as a strong signal, not an absolute guarantee.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Uploaded videos are analyzed and then discarded. The page is built for privacy-first verification without requiring an account.
How does a deepfake video detection tool work?
You upload a video, the AI scans frames for fake artifacts, extracts audio for voice analysis, and returns a combined score with a readable explanation.
What types of content can this tool detect?
It can scan interviews, social clips, screen recordings, and suspicious resume videos. MP4, MOV, and AVI work best with the current backend.
