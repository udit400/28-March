How It Works

Deepfake Detection uses deep learning models trained on large datasets of real and synthetic media. When you upload a file, the system analyzes it using multiple detection signals:

Visual Analysis: Pixel-level inspection for manipulation artifacts, GAN fingerprints, inconsistent lighting, and unnatural facial features.

Audio Analysis: Spectral feature extraction to detect voice cloning patterns, synthesis artifacts, and unnatural speech cadence.

Metadata Forensics: EXIF data inspection, AI provenance marker detection (C2PA), and software platform identification.

Biological Liveness: Multi-frame analysis of facial micro-movements and physiological signals to verify human presence in videos.
