Meet The Team Behind Deepfake Detection

About Us

We built Deepfake Detection to help ordinary people, researchers, educators, journalists, and security teams decide what is real and what is synthetic without needing specialist tools.

Hi! We're a team of AI researchers and cybersecurity experts

We kept asking the same question: how do we help people identify fake content in a world flooded with AI-generated media?

Our Story

Deepfakes became easier to make long before most people had a reliable way to check them. We wanted a tool that felt fast, understandable, and useful in real situations instead of something built only for labs or enterprise demos.

So we focused on three things: clear uploads, readable reports, and privacy-conscious analysis. That turned into a public-facing detector for images, videos, and voice clips that anyone can use without a long setup process.

Our goal is simple: make authenticity checking part of everyday digital hygiene.

What Makes Us Different

We do not just output a single score. The system is designed to surface context so users can understand why something was flagged.

Lightning Fast Analysis

Most scans return quickly enough to fit into live reviews, newsroom checks, or scam-response workflows.

Detailed Reports

Scores are paired with plain-language summaries and supporting detail instead of opaque technical output alone.

Advanced AI Models

We combine multiple signals across visual, acoustic, and metadata patterns to improve trust in the result.

For Everyone

The interface is meant to work for classrooms, households, editorial teams, investigators, and general users.

Our Mission

Truth matters. In an era of digital manipulation, we want more people to have access to tools that reduce uncertainty before a fake spreads.

Protect

Help people avoid scams, impersonation attempts, and manipulated media designed to trigger bad decisions.

Educate

Make deepfake detection easier to understand so users learn how synthetic media is created and caught.

Empower

Keep core detection flows accessible so more people can verify suspicious content before trusting it.

Reach Further

Support individuals and teams across journalism, education, public safety, and personal verification use cases.

Privacy First

Your privacy is central to the product direction. We want users to feel safe checking sensitive audio, photos, and video clips, so the experience is designed around limited retention, secure processing, and readable privacy expectations.

For Truth Seekers Everywhere

Whether you are verifying news, protecting your identity, checking suspicious media for your family, or teaching digital literacy, this platform is meant to be a practical first stop.
